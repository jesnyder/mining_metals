Survey of Metals Cited in NSF Awards on Heavy Metal Remediation

Motivation: Survey the number of awards and amount of funding historically dedicated by NSF to heavy metal remediation.

Objective & Tasks

The objective is to query, scrape, and analyze the NSF awards for trends in funding.

The tasks to complete are:

  1. Query: Pool awards by NSF relevant to "heavy metal public health" and "heavy metal remediation"
  2. Aggregate: Coregister datasets into a consistent, machine readable file structure
  3. Define: Define the size of the database. Plot the number of patents per year (cumulative).
  4. Geolocation: Define the geographic footprint of the database. Map the assignee address of the patents per year.
  5. Targeted Text Analysis: Plot the occurrances of metals in the award database and total the amount awarded to date.
    6. *** not yet completed ***
  6. Untargeted Text Analysis: Count the frequency of all words used in the database.
  7. Cross-reference Google Scholar for cited-by metric to identify most impactful work.
